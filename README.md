☕ Coffee Shop Landing Page:
A simple page for a modern coffee shop, featuring an integrated AI assistant to help customers with orders and FAQs.

✨ Feature:
AI Barista Chatbot: An embedded Botpress bot that handles customer queries in real-time.

🛠️ Technologies Used:
HTML5/CSS3: Structured and styled with a warm, "cafe-vibes" palette.
Botpress: Powering the intelligent webchat interface.

Botpress Integration:
The bot is embedded using the Botpress Webchat script.

How to update the bot:
- Go to your Botpress Cloud Dashboard.
- Navigate to Webchat > Deploy Settings.
- Copy your unique Embedded Code.
- Replace the script tags at the bottom of index.html with your new snippet.
  
Security Note: To prevent unauthorized use of your bot on other websites, ensure you add your domain to the Allowed Origins list in the Botpress settings.
